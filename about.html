About True Cover

Who I am

My name is Joerg Proeve. I've spent 20 years in the insurance industry, but not as a typical broker. My career started on the carrier and product side: underwriting, claims, system architecture, and building insurance products from scratch.

At Chubb and CNA, I worked on the systems that carriers use to price and manage policies. As COO of an insurance startup, I helped build a cyber insurance product from the ground up. I started my career as an engineer, working in cybersecurity.

My background means I understand the risks professional services firms face from both sides: the operational risks your practice carries, and how carriers price these risks.

Why True Cover exists

Too many professional services firms get sold generic insurance by brokers who don't truly understand their operations. The insurance policy says one thing. The practice does another. When a claim happens - and that is the whole point of buying insurance - the gap between the two becomes obvious.

An accountant's E&O policy that defines "professional services" as "tax preparation" when you also do bookkeeping and advisory work. The law firm's cyber policy with a $50K sublimit on business interruption. The consultant's D&O that excludes regulatory investigations. And so on.

Unfortunately, these are not outliers, but the norm. Most brokers never catch them because they never read the policy.

True Cover's approach

I listen. I read. I analyze. I read the policy language, I cross-reference a policy with your operations and contracts. I place coverage that actually matches your needs.

That's what True Cover means. Coverage you can count on because someone checked.
