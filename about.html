About True Cover

Why True Cover exists

Too many professional services firms get sold generic insurance by brokers who don't understand their operations. The insurance policy says one thing. The practice does another. When a claim happens, the gap between the two becomes obvious.

An accountant's E&O policy that defines "professional services" as "tax preparation" when you also do bookkeeping and advisory work. A law firm's cyber policy with a $50K sublimit on business interruption. A consultant's D&O that excludes regulatory investigations.

These are not outliers. Most brokers never catch them because they never read the policy.

True Cover's approach

I listen. I read. I analyze. I read the policy language, cross-reference it with your operations and contracts, and place coverage that actually matches your needs.

That's what True Cover means. Coverage you can count on because someone checked.

Who I am

My name is Joerg Proeve. 20 years in insurance, starting on the carrier and product side: underwriting, claims, system architecture, and building insurance products from scratch. I started my career as an engineer in cybersecurity. That background means I read policy definitions the way most people read code.
