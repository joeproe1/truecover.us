About True Cover

Why True Cover exists

Too many professional services firms get sold generic insurance by brokers who don't understand their operations. The insurance policy says one thing. The practice does another. When a claim happens, the gap between the two becomes obvious.

E&O mismatch. An accountant's policy defines "professional services" as "tax preparation" when you also do bookkeeping and advisory work.
Cyber sublimit. A law firm's cyber policy with a $50K sublimit on business interruption.
D&O gap. A consultant's D&O that excludes regulatory investigations.

These are not outliers. Most brokers never catch them because they never read the policy.
