About True Cover

True Cover is a commercial insurance brokerage for technology companies. Cyber Liability, Technology Errors and Omissions, Directors and Officers, and full commercial programs for software companies, IT services firms, managed service providers, and FinTechs. Licensed in Pennsylvania, Florida, and New Jersey.

Who I am

I'm Joerg Proeve. I have spent 20 years in insurance, but not as a typical broker. My career has been on the carrier and product side: underwriting, claims, system architecture, and building insurance products from scratch.

At Chubb and CNA, I worked on the systems that carriers use to price and manage policies. At BOXX Insurance, I helped build a cyber insurance product from the ground up as COO. Before insurance, I was an engineer at Siemens, working in cybersecurity.

That background means I understand technology companies from the inside, not just from the insurance application. And I understand insurance policies at the language level, not just the price level.

Why True Cover exists

Technology companies get sold insurance by brokers who do not understand their operations. The policy says one thing. The business does another. When a claim happens, the gap between the two is where coverage fails.

The Tech E&O policy that defines "professional services" as "IT consulting" when you actually do security monitoring. The cyber policy with a $50,000 sublimit on business interruption when your downtime costs $200,000 a day. The D&O policy that excludes regulatory investigations when your industry is regulated.

These are not edge cases. They are the norm. And they happen because most brokers compare price, not coverage.

The approach

I read the policy language. I cross-reference it against your operations and contracts. I place coverage that matches what your business does, not what the application said two years ago.

That is what True Cover means. Coverage you can count on because someone checked.
