Commercial Insurance

BOP, general liability, workers compensation, EPLI, commercial auto, and umbrella coverage. The foundation of your full insurance program.

Standard commercial coverage

Business Owners Policy (BOP) / General Liability

Combines commercial property and general liability. Covers office space, equipment, and liability for bodily injury or property damage to third parties.

Workers Compensation

Required in most states once you have employees. Covers medical expenses and lost wages for on-the-job injuries.

Employment Practices Liability (EPLI)

Covers claims from employees alleging wrongful termination, discrimination, harassment, or retaliation. Increasingly important as your team grows.

Commercial Auto

Liability and physical damage coverage if your company owns vehicles or employees drive for work purposes.

Umbrella / Excess Liability

Additional limits above your primary policies. For technology companies with large client contracts, umbrella coverage is often required to meet contractual minimums.
